8. IMPORTING INTO GODOT
----------------------------------------

  • FBX: Drag .fbx into FileSystem. Godot imports automatically. Use for Mixamo/Kenney FBX.
  • glTF/GLB: Native. Drag into project. Best for Blender exports.
  • OBJ: Supported. Drag into project.
  • Textures: Import as Texture2D. Assign to StandardMaterial3D (albedo, normal, etc.).
  • Audio: .wav or .ogg in project. Use in AudioStreamPlayer nodes.

  Mixamo → Godot: Export FBX from Mixamo (With Skin, 30 FPS). Import in Godot. For animations, see: https://docs.godotengine.org/en/stable/tutorials/assets_pipeline/importing_scenes.html (skeleton retargeting).
